Privacy Policy for Vaannilai Seithigal

Last updated: September 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Vaannilai Seithigal collects minimal information to provide weather services:

  • Location Data: We may access your device's location to provide local weather information (only with your permission)
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide accurate weather forecasts for your location
  • Send weather alerts and notifications
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Analyze app usage patterns (anonymized)

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your location data with third parties
  • Store personal identifiable information

We DO share:

  • Anonymous usage statistics with analytics providers (PostHog)
  • Weather data requests with weather API providers

4. Location Services

Location access is optional and used only to:

  • Detect your current district in Tamil Nadu
  • Provide relevant local weather information
  • Send location-specific weather alerts

You can disable location access anytime in your device settings.

5. Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • No personal data is stored permanently on our servers
  • Weather data is cached temporarily for app performance

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • WeatherAPI.com: For weather data (subject to their privacy policy)
  • PostHog: For anonymous analytics (subject to their privacy policy)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Users will be notified of significant changes within the app.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions:

  • Email: privacy@vaannilai-seithigal.com
  • App Support: Available within the app settings

10. Tamil Nadu Specific

This app is designed specifically for Tamil Nadu residents and complies with Indian data protection guidelines and regulations.

வானிலை செய்திகள் - Your privacy is important to us. This app is designed to serve the Tamil Nadu community with respect for your personal information.
